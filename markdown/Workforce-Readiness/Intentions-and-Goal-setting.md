# Intentions and Goal-setting 

Instructions: Write at least one complete sentence per question.

1. What do you want to do as a career and why?
2. What do you want to get out of this class? Write a goal for yourself.
3. What do you need from yourself to be successful in this class and achieve your goal?
4. What do you need from me to be successful in this class and achieve your goal?
5. I will know I have been successful in class everyday if/when ___?


## Rubric
- **8 points** - All answers have been completed thoughtfully
- **6 points** - All answers have been completed but lack depth
- **4 points** - Missing or incomplete
