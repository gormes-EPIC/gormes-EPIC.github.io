# Creating a Resume Assignment

## Part 1: First Draft Resume
1. Find an entry level computer science job, internship, scholarship, or college program you qualify for. 
2. List the top three(at least) most important skills or features or values you see on the description that the resume reviewer would be looking for.
3. Create a resume that includes the three required features you just identified. Create a copy of either of the following templates or create your own. Feel free to delete or add sections based on what is relevant to you. See these examples:
    - [Basic](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1qm103aqyyXkENiH15nFXJBZE0PQtb3YibK1bSr2cJHg/edit?usp=sharing)
    - [Detailed](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1w9u1LEldQ4AOLZ1DEt_-T_Q4v1h8wVvSxAcVJXx85UE/edit?usp=sharing)
4. Submit your resume, your three skills, and a link to your job/scholarship/internship/college program on Google Classroom.
 
## Part 2: Reviewing Feedback
1. Review the feedback about your resume and make the appropriate changes.
2. Resubmit your resume

## Rubric
- **30 points** - Resume is complete and appropriately complements job description
- **25 points** - Resume is complete but lacks detail. Doesn't address aspects of the job description, bullet points are general, is poorly formatted etc.
- **20 points** - Resume is missing sections, has grammatical errors, doesn't address comments, etc.
- **15 points** - Completed part 1, but did not complete part 2
- **0 points** - Incomplete or missing
