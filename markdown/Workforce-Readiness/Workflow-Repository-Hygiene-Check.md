# Workflow Repository Hygiene Check

*This assignment is under construction and is subject to change for the 26-27 school year.* 

## Objective
1. Review and get feedback on your technical writing

## Your Task

1. Get the link to your semester project repository and switch projects with someone in the class. 
2. Each of you will [complete this rubric](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1dqx9Sib6L7wRoG8D87osj-BQz9VtIv6KVUwt0jAMrew/edit?usp=sharing) based on each other's repository.
3. Based on their feedback, come up with three actionable changes you can make to improve your written documentation.

## Rubric
- **30 points** - Your peer rubric is complete. Your three changes are actionable and adhere to your goals for your repositories.  
- **15 points** - Your peer rubric is complete but you did not include three actionable changes based on their feedback and your personal goals.
- **0 points** - Missing or incomplete
