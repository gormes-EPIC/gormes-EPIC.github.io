# Design Review Discussion

*This assignment is under construction and is subject to change for the 26-27 school year.* 

## Objective
1. Present and defend your design decisions
2. Cite effective design principles 

## What to Prepare

Please be prepared to answer the following questions about your project: 

1. What data did you need to represent and how does your database schema effectively model the information?
2. What other schema did you consider and why did you choose this specific structure? How does your databse reflect effective database design principles?
3. Why did you choose these specific API endpoints? How do they expose only the necessary information to the outside world?
4. How does your design prevent expliotation via security flaws?
5. How have you documented your project so far?
