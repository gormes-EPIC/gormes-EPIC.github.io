# Working with Raspberry Pis Notes

In a complete circuit, electrons flow from the negative side of the battery to the positive side of a battery. 

<img src="/assets/working-with-pis-figure-1.png">

The **anode** is the positive side of the battery and the **cathode** is the negative side.

<img src="/assets/working-with-pis-figure-2.png">

The anode of the battery connects to the anode of the light and the cathode of the battery connects to the cathode of the light.


### Key Point: Connect anode to anode and cathode to cathode
